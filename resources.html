    N. R. Baker – “10:59” (2020)

    As a fiction book that tackles the issue of overpopulation, 10:59 centers around Louis Crawford, a teenager who early in is his life realizes the problems that come with an ever growing human population. When he finds himself working for an organization intent on saving the world, he has to choose how far he is willing to go to save humanity and the planet. 10:59 is a gripping book that explores the personal effects of overpopulation while not shying away from the larger problems surrounding it. Especially valuable is the short chapter at the end of the story, the Author’s notes, which further explains the reasoning behind the book and ensures the reader understands that while the story is a work of fiction, overpopulation is not.
